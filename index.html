ABOUT US

ENACTUS is an international non-profit organization dedicated to inspiring students to improve the world through entrepreneurial action. A community of student, academic and business leaders committed to using the power of entrepreneurial action to transform lives and shape a better, more sustainable world.

ENtrepreneurial: initiating business ideas and solutions with passion and innovation. ACTion: The experience of sustainable impact, activated with integrity. US: Student, academic and business leaders collaborating to create a better world. Enactus is spread across 36 countries and partnered with over 1700 Universities empowering 1,950,000+ people
